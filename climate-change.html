Climate Change

Sub-topic: Impacts

The impacts include more frequent and intense weather events like hurricanes and wildfires, rising sea levels, disruption to agriculture, and threats to biodiversity and human health.

Sub-topic: Mitigation

Mitigation strategies focus on reducing greenhouse gas emissions. This involves transitioning to renewable energy (solar, wind), improving energy efficiency, and protecting forests.

Sub-topic: Adaptation

Adaptation means adjusting to the current and future effects of climate change. This includes building resilient infrastructure, developing drought-resistant crops, and managing water resources.
